Privacy Policy for Expose Food App

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

Expose Food App (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Android application that scans barcodes to provide detailed information about food and cosmetic products (“the App”).
By using Expose Food App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect
1.1 Personal Information

Expose Food App does NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email, phone number, address, or login details.

1.2 Barcode Scan Data

When you scan a barcode using the App:

The barcode value is processed locally on your device or sent securely to a product database/API to retrieve product information.

Barcode data is not stored, not tracked, and not linked to your identity.

We do not create user profiles or history of scanned items.

1.3 Device & Technical Data (Non-Personal)

To improve app performance, we may collect anonymous technical data such as:

Device type and model

Operating system version

App version

Crash logs and diagnostics

This data cannot identify you and is used purely for app improvement.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the information strictly for:

Displaying product information for food and cosmetic items

Improving app functionality, accuracy, and performance

Debugging issues and ensuring app stability

Enhancing user experience

We never sell, rent, or share your information for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

Expose Food App may use trusted third-party services such as:

Product information databases

Ingredient & nutritional data sources

Crash-reporting tools (e.g., Firebase Crashlytics)

These services may process technical data or barcode values but never receive personal information, because we do not collect any.

You can refer to their privacy policies for more details.

4. Data Security

We do not store scanned barcode data or personal information.

All requests to external APIs are encrypted when transmitted.

Technical data used for debugging is handled securely following industry standards.

Your privacy and safety are our top priorities.

5. Children’s Privacy

Expose Food App does not target children under the age of 13.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If you believe a child has provided us with any information, please contact us and we will remove it immediately.

6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data:

You do not need to request data deletion

You do not need to request data correction

You do not need to request data export

However, you may contact us anytime if you have questions about your privacy.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
When changes are made, we will update the “Last Updated” date at the top.
Continued use of Expose Food App means you accept the updated terms.
